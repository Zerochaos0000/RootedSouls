What Is Rooted Soles?

A Mission That
Grows With Every Pair

Rooted Soles was founded on a simple idea: our old sneakers shouldn't end up in a landfill. They should help grow a tree. We collect donated footwear, sell it to partner organizations, and send every dollar to Deep Roots Foundation — a 501(c)(3) nonprofit planting trees across Delaware.


No overhead fluff. No bureaucracy. Just shoes in, trees out.

Our Story

One Founder.
One Partner. One Mission.

Rooted Soles was founded by Aadhya. Deep Roots Foundation — our tree-planting partner — was founded by her brother Dhruv. Together they form a two-organization engine that turns donated shoes into planted trees.

🌱
Founder & CEO · Rooted Soles
Aadhya Pratapagiri

I started Rooted Soles because I kept seeing perfectly good shoes get thrown away. I thought — what if we connected that waste to something meaningful? One pair of shoes, one tree. That idea felt worth building.

FAQ

Got
Questions?

We've got answers. If yours isn't here, use the contact form above.

What types of shoes do you accept?
▾
We accept all types of sneakers and footwear in any condition. We sort donations and send them to our partner organizations who pay us directly per pair.
Where do I drop off my shoes?
▾
We run collection drives at schools and community events across Delaware. Use the contact form to find the nearest drop-off or arrange a pickup for larger donations.
How does the money actually reach the trees?
▾
Partner organizations pay Rooted Soles for every pair of shoes we send them. That money flows directly to Deep Roots Foundation, a registered 501(c)(3), which purchases and plants native Delaware trees.
Can my school host a collection drive?
▾
Absolutely — school partnerships are our favorite! We provide collection boxes, flyers, and full guidance. Just reach out through the contact form to get started.
Is my financial donation tax-deductible?
▾
Financial donations to Deep Roots Foundation are tax-deductible as it is a registered 501(c)(3). We can provide documentation upon request.
How can I follow your progress?
▾
Check back on this site for updates on trees planted, shoes collected, and upcoming events. You can also sign up for updates through the contact form.
